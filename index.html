The Protocol

Five steps to a healthier scalp

Every visit is built around a clear diagnostic-to-treatment sequence, so you always know what's being done and why.

01

Detailed Microscope Analysis

A high-magnification look at your scalp and follicles to identify buildup, inflammation, miniaturization or the true source of shedding before any treatment plan is built.

02

Red Light Laser

Low-level laser therapy delivers targeted light energy to stimulate circulation at the follicle and support the hair growth cycle.

03

High Frequency

Gentle high-frequency current helps cleanse the scalp, calm irritation and encourage a balanced, healthy follicle environment.

04

Scalp Massage

Hands-on massage relieves tension, improves microcirculation, and gives your scalp the same care your skin gets in a facial.

05

Customized Scalp Treatment

A tailored formula — matched to what your microscope analysis actually shows — to address scalp disorders, hair loss, and to unlock your fullest growth potential.
